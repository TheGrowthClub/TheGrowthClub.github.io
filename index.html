Course Structure

A1 Beginner
Days 1-60
Foundation level: Basic greetings, simple sentences, present tense, common vocabulary. Perfect for absolute beginners starting their English journey.
A2 Elementary
Days 61-120
Building skills: Past tense, future tense, common phrases, everyday situations. Express simple ideas and handle basic conversations.
B1 Intermediate
Days 121-180
Developing fluency: Complex sentences, conditionals, opinions, storytelling. Handle most everyday situations and express thoughts clearly.
B2 Upper-Intermediate
Days 181-240
Advanced communication: Nuanced expression, idioms, formal writing, complex discussions. Understand main ideas of complex texts and interact fluently.
C1 Advanced
Days 241-300
Near-native proficiency: Sophisticated vocabulary, literary analysis, professional communication. Express ideas fluently and spontaneously without searching for words.
C2 Mastery
Days 301-365
Native-level mastery: Advanced idioms, cultural references, academic writing, professional expertise. Understand virtually everything and express yourself with precision.

Level-Specific Channels

Each level has its own dedicated channel with cycling content throughout the day. Level channel commands work within their respective channels:

!stats View level-specific progress
!today Today's lesson preview for this level
!help Show commands for this level

Daily Lesson Includes

  • 5 New Vocabulary Words With definitions, examples, and usage tips
  • Grammar Topic Structured grammar lessons building on previous knowledge
  • Practice Sentences Real-world application of new words and grammar
  • Reading Passage Level-appropriate texts to build comprehension

Political Debate Arena

Premium

How It Works:

Post your political questions or topics in the Political Discussion channel to receive perspectives from four diverse AI debate agents. Each agent represents a different political viewpoint and will engage with your topic from their unique ideological stance.

Meet the Debate Agents

Viktor
Conservative Realist

Traditionalist perspective emphasizing order, hierarchy, national sovereignty, and proven institutions. Values stability, cultural preservation, and skepticism of rapid change.

Zara
Progressive Activist

Social justice advocate focusing on equality, systemic reform, environmental action, and human rights. Champions marginalized voices and transformative social change.

Arjun
Pragmatic Centrist

Evidence-based moderate seeking practical solutions and compromise. Values data-driven policy, incremental reform, and finding common ground across ideological divides.

Rex
Libertarian Individualist

Maximizes individual liberty and minimal government intervention. Emphasizes free markets, personal responsibility, and skepticism of state power and regulation.

Usage Tips:
  • Post thoughtful questions to get the best multi-perspective responses
  • Agents will debate each other, creating dynamic discussions
  • Use this to understand different viewpoints on complex political issues
  • Perfect for developing critical thinking and nuanced understanding
